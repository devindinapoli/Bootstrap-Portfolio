About Me

My Name is Devin DiNapoli, and I'm a pastry-chef turned web developer. Making pastries was a very precise science - if something wasn't exactly the way it should be, the whole thing would be ruined. It's also an art - I could make an ordinary dessert and turn into a picture perfect dish. It was a challenge, but I needed something more challenging than that. Making websites into art and a science is my biggest challenge yet, a challenge that I'm perfecting one step at a time.
